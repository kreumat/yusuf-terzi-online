Founding Members Wanted: Bursa Uludağ University ELT Society

July 10, 2025
This opportunity is still ongoing! You can still join as a founding member.

About the Society

We’re starting something exciting at Uludağ University — the ELT Society! This will be a space for students who care about English language teaching to come together, share ideas, and build something meaningful. Whether you want to grow as a future teacher or just enjoy being part of a fun, creative community, this society is for you.

Who Can Join

If you're a student at Uludağ University and you're either studying ELT or simply interested in language, teaching, or communication — you're more than welcome to join. You don’t have to be from the ELT department to be part of the team.

Why Join

There are lots of reasons to get involved:

  • Meet people who share your interests
  • Take part in events that help you grow personally and professionally
  • Be active in university life and make the most of your time here
  • Add something meaningful to your CV
  • And most importantly — have fun while doing it!

What Kind of Events Will We Have?

We’re planning a mix of events that are both useful and fun:

  • Social events like movie nights, conversation clubs, and game nights
  • Creative and hands-on events like storytelling, material design, and micro-teaching
  • Professional events like workshops, guest talks, and CV-prep sessions

Founding Member Medal

If you join now as one of our first members, you’ll be part of the society’s official founding team — and to celebrate that, you’ll get an exclusive Founding Member Medal as a keepsake.

Pioneer Member

Awarded to the first 30 founding members

How to Join

It’s super easy — just click the WhatsApp icon below, join our WhatsApp group, and contact one of the admins in the group chat. If you have any questions, feel free to ask there. We’d love to have you with us!
