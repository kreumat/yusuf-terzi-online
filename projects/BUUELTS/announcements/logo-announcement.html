Be Part of Our Identity: Design Our Society Logo

July 9, 2025
This is still your chance to join as a founding logo designer — submissions are still open!

Call for Logo Submissions

Our society is just getting started, and we’re looking for a logo that can proudly represent who we are from day one. If you enjoy graphic design and want to contribute to something meaningful, this is your opportunity to help shape the main logo of our society — potentionally one that will be used for many years to come.

Before submitting your design, please carefully read and follow official guidelines ("yönerge") on how student societies can use logos.

Official Guidelines for Society Logos — Yönerge

MADDE 18/A- (1) ÖT’nin logo kullanmalarına ilişkin esaslar aşağıdaki gibidir: Senato Karar Eki
a) ÖT, Üniversitenin kurumsal kimliğine uygun bir topluluk logosu tasarlayarak, onay işlemleri için Daire Başkanlığı’na sunarlar.
b) Logo taslakları ön inceleme için Daire Başkanlığı tarafından ÖEK’ya gönderilir; uygun görülen logolar onay için Senatoya sunulur.
c) Senato tarafından onaylanan logolar, ÖT etkinliklerinin duyurulmasında ve topluluğu tanıtıcı her türlü materyalde kullanılabilir.

Shortly, student societies must design a logo fitting the university’s identity, get Senate approval, and only approved logos can be used officially. It is expected that the logo be circular in shape, similar to the university’s own logo.

Important: Please send your logo design only to the provided email address. Do not send your design directly to the Senate. After we select the logo, we will handle the submission to the Senate for approval.

Submission Deadline and Email

Submit to: 062310053@ogr.uludag.edu.tr
Deadline: September 1, 2025

Get a medal for participating!

Everyone who submits a logo design — even if their design is not officially accepted — will receive a “Logo Contributor” medal on our website in recognition of their creative effort.
